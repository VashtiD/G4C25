PITCH

Jump into the colorful ocean world of *Trash to Treasure*, where you can be a hero by cleaning up the sea! Choose and customize your character, then get ready for some action! Use easy controls to move around the ocean and race against the clock to collect as much trash as you can in just 10 seconds. With beautiful underwater graphics, *Trash to Treasure* not only challenges you but also teaches the importance of protecting our environment. Earn cool rewards like starfish and shells as you try to beat your high score!

THEME

In *Trash to Treasure*, players go on an adventure to clean up the ocean after a big storm has left it covered in trash. Through fun conversations with friendly sea creatures, players learn about how pollution affects marine life and how they can help protect their home. Short cutscenes show the ocean's story and help players understand why they need to care for the environment. The game is filled with colorful underwater scenes and lively fish, which really highlight the contrast between the beautiful ocean and the trash that needs cleaning up. As players collect trash, they earn cool rewards that let them upgrade their characters and get special skills. Racing against the clock helps remind everyone how important it is to keep the ocean clean and make a difference.

MY WHY

Creating a game like "Trash to Treasure" can be a powerful tool for change. It not only entertains but also encourages players to be more environmentally conscious and inspires them to take positive actions towards protecting the planet. In short, it reflects the belief that games can be a catalyst for awareness, education, and community building in addressing some of today’s most pressing challenges.
